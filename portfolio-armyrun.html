Canada Army Run

Visit armyrun.ca

Role
Web Design, UX / UI, Photography, Graphic Design

Canada Army Run is more than just a race. It's a community, a camaraderie, and it supports Canadian Armed Forces members and their families. Canada Army Run is an annual in-person and virtual running event acting as an extension of the Canadian Armed Forces (CAF), showcasing itself to connect with Canadians across the country and worldwide. Since 2008, Canada Army Run has raised over $4.4 million for its two charitable causes: Support Our Troops and Soldier On â€“ helping military families build resiliency and providing assistance to ill and injured members.

The primary challenge faced by Canada Army Run was to increase its registration numbers, enhance its brand value, tell its story to inspire participants to get involved in fundraising initiatives through web design and improved UX and UI. The website needed to achieve a higher level of audience engagement, boost online visibility, and turn new visitors into returning ones and loyal customers. There was a need to make the pages mobile-friendly, enhanced SEO, meta tags and update of the website structure. I created signage for the 2024 in-person Ottawa event using the updated Canadian Armed Forces colors and camouflage pattern. I attended and photographed the 2023 and 2024 Canada Army Run in-person Ottawa events, using a Canon 5D Mark IV paired with a Sigma 85mm f/1.4 DG HSM Art Lens, using the photographs for the website slider imagery and in several of its social media campaigns.

Conclusion

I integrated the Canadian Armed Forces updated branding initiative featuring new camouflage pattern and colours with the Canada Army Run website design aesthetic, retaining the integrity of the more than decade old brand while updating the interface to better reflect its contemporary user base.

2024 saw a 20% increase in Organic Search, and 42% increase in Direct Search results on the Canada Army Run website. Improved SEO, correct tagging and configuration, implementing cross-domain tracking and handling redirects and referrals properly resulted in a 600% reduction in Unassigned Traffic. Canada Army Run registration numbers increased by 17% thereby increasing its brand value.
