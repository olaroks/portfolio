A bit about me

When I was a little girl, I always dreamed of growing up to satisfy user needs in a way that meets business goals for transformative outcomes.

I make good things happen

Web Designer

As a full-stack web designer, I take ownership of the entire web development process, from initial concept through to final implementation. I bridge the gap between design and development, ensuring that the visual aesthetics and functionality of a website are fully aligned with both the client’s vision and the user’s needs. On any given project, I focus on creating intuitive, engaging interfaces while also handling the back-end architecture that drives performance, security, and scalability.

My work begins with understanding the user experience and creating design prototypes that reflect the brand’s identity and goals. Once the design is established, I move into the development phase, where I build the front-end interfaces with responsive design principles and implement the back-end logic that powers features like databases, APIs, or authentication systems.

Balancing creativity with technical expertise, I ensure that the sites I create are not only visually compelling but also robust, and optimized for performance across different platforms. Whether I’m working on a small business website or a complex, data-driven platform, I’m focused on delivering a cohesive, high-quality solution that integrates design and development seamlessly.

Digital Marketer

As a digital marketer, I focus on crafting and executing strategies that drive brand awareness, engagement, and conversions across digital platforms. I develop campaigns that align with a company’s objectives, whether that’s increasing traffic, generating leads, or boosting online sales. I leverage a mix of content creation, social media management, and targeted advertising to engage with the right audiences at the right time.

I ensure that websites are optimized for search engines, increasing their visibility through organic search rankings. I also manage paid search campaigns to maximize return on investment and drive immediate results. The combination of SEO and SEM helps brands reach their audience more effectively, whether through natural discovery or targeted advertising.

I consistently monitor the performance of campaigns to track key metrics—such as click-through rates, conversion rates, and user behavior. By diving deep into the data, I can identify what’s working and where adjustments need to be made, helping to fine-tune our approach over time.

I create measurable, scalable marketing strategies that not only reach the target audience but also drive meaningful results for the business.

Creative Project Manager

As a creative project manager, I oversee the entire production process from concept to completion, ensuring that projects are delivered on time, on budget, and aligned with the client’s vision. I manage the workflow, coordinate teams, and bridge communication between stakeholders—whether that’s designers, developers, marketing teams, or external vendors.

Working closely with cross-functional teams, I guide the creative process, ensuring that the brand’s identity, messaging, and objectives are consistently reflected in every deliverable. Collaboration is key here: I work with vendors and external partners—whether that’s freelance designers, production agencies, or technical specialists—to align on project requirements, negotiate contracts, and ensure everyone is working toward the same goal.

I understand the technical and creative aspects of a project and anticipate challenges, ensuring that every piece—from website design to digital campaigns—fits cohesively into the larger brand strategy.

Ultimately, my job is to bring all the moving parts together, driving successful outcomes that stay on budget, meet deadlines, and maintain the project's creative integrity.
