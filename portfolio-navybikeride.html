Navy Bike Ride

Visit navybikeride.ca

Role
Web Design, UX / UI, Digital Marketing, Photography, Graphic Design, Motion Graphics, Video Editing

Established in 2016, the Navy Bike Ride promotes the well-being of communities and families through cycling, whether in physical or virtual settings, raising money for three charities: Support Our Troops, Soldier On and the Royal Canadian Naval Benevolent Fund. Objectives on the project were to promote NBR registration and fundraising by NBR participants and inform about the important work done by SOT, Solider On, and RCNBF. The event exists to connect the RCN with Canadians and reinforce that connection with our communities, promote RCN history and heritage, and showcase NBR sponsors. I produced a Communications Plan, comprehensive Editorial Calendar covering all social media and email marketing channels in addition to various digital and print marketing materials promoting the event.

In 2023, the Naval Reserve celebrated a major milestone marking 100 years of service to Canada. The Royal Canadian Navy branded the occasion in navy blue, yellow and white colours, producing emblems and imagery which I introduced into the website redesign and marketing materials ensuring that all assets adhered to the RCNâ€™s branding guidelines.

I re-designed the Navy Bike Ride website to better suit the mobile viewing audience, using an endless scrolling design, embedding clear calls to action throughout, creating customized graphic overlays, highlighting the past successful events with curated photo series.

I made templates and attention-grabbing posters for social media ads targeting families in key Ottawa, Halifax, Esquimalt and Quebec City metro areas. I created a series of high-resolution posters, billboard ads, and flyers highlighting the event. The flyers promoted the event launches across Canada on Instagram reels/stories, and PPC ads and hashtags helped expand the reach.

Conclusion

Targeted PPC ads led to significant growth across social platforms, with Instagram followers increasing by 375% and reach by 45%. On Facebook, page likes surged by 500%, while reach skyrocketed by an impressive 9,500%
